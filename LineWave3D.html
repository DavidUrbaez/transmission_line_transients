Traveling waves on a transmission line

What am I looking at? The cable bends to the instantaneous voltage profile V(x, tnow) as the wave sweeps along it. Turn on Show 3D surface to unfold the full f(x, t) “spacetime carpet” — the wake the wave leaves trailing behind its front.

Reading it

A slice across x is the “grandstand snapshot” f(x−vt); a slice down t is the “racetrack-crack” view f(x/v−t). Ridges are Bewley’s lattice diagonals.

Things to try

  • Set the far end to Open and watch the reflection nearly double the voltage (Ferranti).
  • Toggle Show 3D surface to see the wake build up behind the wavefront.
  • Use the A/B overlay to compare Lossless against Frequency-Dependent.
  • Switch the waveform to a Switching impulse or Sinusoidal source.
